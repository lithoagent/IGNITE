# Glossary

## ADL
Auto-deleveraging. A fallback mechanism used when liquidation and insurance are insufficient to absorb system losses.

## Batch
A settlement unit containing a set of deterministic state deltas committed on-chain.

## Cross Margin
A margin mode where account equity is shared across positions.

## Funding Rate
A periodic transfer between longs and shorts designed to anchor perpetual prices to spot.

## IM
Initial margin. Minimum collateral required to open or expand a position.

## MM
Maintenance margin. Minimum margin ratio required to avoid liquidation.

## Mark Price
The risk reference price used for margin and liquidation calculations.

## Notional
Absolute position size multiplied by mark price.

## State Delta
A compact description of the balance and position changes resulting from executed trades.

## Vault
A smart contract-based collateral container that tracks balances and locked margin.
