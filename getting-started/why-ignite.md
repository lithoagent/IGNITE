# Why Ignite DEX

Most decentralized exchanges force a tradeoff between performance and trust minimization. Ignite is designed to reduce that tradeoff.

## What Ignite optimizes for

### Speed

Professional traders expect low latency, deterministic fills, and real-time market data. Ignite is built around an in-memory matching engine, high-throughput streaming, and batched settlement.

### Custody

Ignite does not require users to hand over assets to a centralized custodian. Funds remain in smart contract vaults and can be withdrawn subject to margin constraints.

### Chain abstraction

Users should not need to think about four separate execution environments to use one exchange. Ignite presents a unified trading system while maintaining explicit chain-level settlement guarantees.

### Extensibility

Ignite is not only an exchange. It is a programmable financial engine with APIs, SDKs, market creation, incentives, and AI coordination.

## Comparison mindset

Ignite follows the design pattern of modern high-performance exchanges: deterministic execution, clean APIs, and deeply integrated market structure. The differentiator is the combination of multi-chain support, permissionless infrastructure, and AI-native components.
