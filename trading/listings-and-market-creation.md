# Listings & Market Creation

Ignite is permissionless by design.

## Listing principles

- anyone can create eligible markets
- anyone can provide liquidity
- governance can define risk and safety parameters
- inactive or unsafe markets can be delisted automatically

## Proposed listing flow

1. deploy or import token
2. select trading pair
3. provide bootstrap liquidity
4. submit the market creation transaction
5. market becomes live

## Safety controls

Markets can be delisted or disabled if:

- liquidity falls below the minimum threshold
- there is no activity for a sustained period
- oracle support becomes unreliable
- governance or guardians intervene during emergency conditions
