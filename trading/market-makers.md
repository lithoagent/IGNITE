# Market Makers

Ignite is designed for professional market making.

## What market makers need

- predictable sequencing
- low latency data streams
- post-only protection
- detailed position and fills data
- stable funding and fee policies

## Infrastructure support

- API keys and session models
- WebSocket user streams
- binary transport for low-latency systems
- broker and referral identifiers where configured

## Incentive model

Market makers can receive rebates or negative fees depending on liquidity programs and volume tiers.
