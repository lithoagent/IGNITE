# Fees

Ignite uses a maker/taker fee model with IGNT at the center of protocol fee flows.

## Baseline ranges

- Maker: 0% or rebate depending on program and market role
- Taker: 0.01% to 0.02% target range depending on product and tier

## Fee settlement

Fees are payable in IGNT across supported networks in the Ignite model. Future flows may support automatic conversion from other assets into IGNT.

## Fee incentives

- market maker rebates
- trading rewards
- referrals
- liquidity mining programs
