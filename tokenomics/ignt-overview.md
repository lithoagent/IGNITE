# IGNT Overview

IGNT is the native utility and coordination token of the Ignite ecosystem.

## Functions

- trading fee asset
- staking asset
- governance asset
- liquidity incentive asset
- selective collateral asset in supported markets

## Multi-chain form

IGNT is designed as a multi-standard, multi-chain asset:

- LEP100 on Lithosphere
- ERC-20 on Base
- ERC-20 on Arbitrum
- ERC-20 on BNB Chain
